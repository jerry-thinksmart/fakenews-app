FakeNews Detector was built to tackle the growing crisis of online misinformation. We use state-of-the-art machine learning to help individuals, journalists, and organizations quickly verify whether a news article is real or fake.

Our platform is trained on thousands of verified news articles, delivering over 98% accuracy so you can trust the results. We believe an informed public is a stronger society.
